# DearLuna — Your Daily Glow 🌙

**DearLuna** is a premium, minimalist self-care and cycle intelligence suite designed to harmonize your daily rituals with your natural rhythms. Handcrafted for tranquility, it serves as a digital sanctuary for your wellness journey.

---

## ✨ How to Explore DearLuna

Welcome to your sanctuary. Here is how to get the most out of your experience:

### 1. The Setup Sanctuary 🌿
When you first launch the app, you'll be greeted by the **Setup Sanctuary**. This is where you configure your cycle data and personalize your dashboard. Take a moment to set your preferences—the app uses this to tailor affirmations and breathing exercises specifically for you.

### 2. The LunaDial (Cycle Intelligence) 🪐
Located in the heart of the app, the **LunaDial** isn't just a tracker—it's a visualization of your energy. 
*   **Explore Phases**: Click through the dial to see how your cycle impacts your energy, mood, and skin.
*   **Daily Insights**: Each phase comes with curated self-care tips and "Daily Glow" tasks.

### 3. The Command Deck 📊
Your central hub for productivity and peace. Use the Command Deck to:
*   View your **Wellness Analytics**.
*   Log your **Daily Rituals** and habits.
*   Check your **Affirmation of the Day**.

### 4. The Memory Vault & Safe Space 📔
*   **Journal**: A quiet space for your thoughts. Everything is stored locally and encrypted.
*   **Scrapbook**: Create digital mood boards using stickers and memories to capture your glow.

## 📦 How to Use (Distribution)

Your download folder contains two versions:

### 🚀 Installer Version (`DearLuna Setup 1.0.0.exe`)
*   **Best for**: Daily use on your main PC.
*   **Benefits**: Creates desktop shortcuts, integrates with your Start Menu, and supports **Automatic Updates**.
*   **How to**: Run the setup and follow the instructions.

### 🎒 Portable Version (`DearLuna 1.0.0.exe`)
*   **Best for**: Privacy on the go or testing.
*   **Benefits**: No installation required. Runs instantly from a USB drive or any folder.
*   **How to**: Just double-click and launch.

## 🛠️ Developer Guide (deepxcoder)

If you are a developer looking to contribute or build from source:

### Prerequisites
*   Node.js (v18+)
*   npm

### Commands
```powershell
# 1. Install dependencies
npm install

# 2. Build and Package for Windows
npm run package
```
*Build artifacts will be automatically organized into the `/Release` folder.*
